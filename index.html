About Me

Hello Meet me, A Computer Science Graduate with Interests in Game Development
with Junior Unity Developer certification from Game Train.

A young guy from the outsourced area of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, trying to persuit carrier in the field of Game Development. As a child I had a deepest passion for playing games and that used to trigger the thinking in me of the mechanics of how the game was developed, then took Computer Science as a course in matriculation and continued the persuit of a perfect field in Computer Science by choosing Computer Science as a Bachelor's Degree Course. Then after completing degree, found this dream course of Junior Game Developer by GameTrain co-powered by Genertaion Pakistan. This was the Game Changing Moment for me as I shifted gears in persuit of what I used to think about in my Childhood.
